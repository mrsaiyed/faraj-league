Who We Are

About the League

The Faraj League is a community basketball league built on brotherhood and the spirit of collective preparation. Our name is drawn from the narration of the Holy Prophet (s.a.w.s.): أفْضَلُ العِبادةِ انتِظارُ الفَرَجْ — "The best worship is awaiting the reappearance."
